Design Comp Summary

I created a color scheme with mostly the color green since green is associated with sustainability and the environment. I did my best to keep the interface simple, with a clear hierarachy of information, in order to make it clear and easy to use. I made the shapes more rounded to have a more friendly, modern-looking appearance.

In subsequent versions, I plan ask people to review my work and incorporate their comments into my work. I also plan to refine the color scheme and balance of color, and of course make some decisions about what exactly I want the text to say.
